## 🏢 About Us

**CipherTronix**, founded in **2011**, has firmly established itself as a premier provider of IT and network security services in Pakistan. With an unwavering commitment to innovation and excellence, we empower businesses to address their most pressing technology and security challenges.

Over the years, the company has grown exponentially, delivering high-value technology solutions that enhance operational efficiency and drive profitability for our clients. What distinguishes CipherTronix from its competitors is the ability to **blend local expertise with global standards** — backed by a richly experienced team drawn from leading multinational and local organizations.

This diverse pool of talent enables us to deliver tailored solutions, whether designing resilient IT infrastructures, implementing advanced security measures, or streamlining operational processes.

### 🔎 eSentinel
**Enterprise Fraud Risk Management System**

<img src="https://ciphertronix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/esentinel-logo-with-background.webp" width="180"/>

eSentinel is a versatile fraud detection and risk monitoring solution for **Issuance, Acquiring, Branchless Banking, and Virtual Wallets**.

It equips fraud analysts with a unified dashboard powered by pre-defined rules tailored to local fraud trends. Designed for **near real-time detection** of suspicious transactions, it leverages a grading matrix and integrates seamlessly with banking systems via **ISO 8583**.

**Key Capabilities**
- 🛡️ Near real-time transaction monitoring
- 📊 Rule-based grading matrix
- 🔗 Payment industry verified
- 🧭 Unified fraud analyst dashboard<br>

### 🚛 STOS
**Smart Terminal Operating System**

<img src="https://ciphertronix.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/STOS-with-background-Copy.webp" width="150"/>

STOS is a centralized platform for managing and operating payment terminal fleets at scale. It streamlines **terminal deployment, configuration, monitoring, and lifecycle management** across distributed networks.

Built for acquirers and financial institutions, STOS delivers secure, remote control over terminal estates while ensuring uptime, compliance, and operational visibility.

**Key Capabilities**
- 📡 Remote terminal management & monitoring
- ⚙️ Centralized configuration & deployment
- 🔐 Secure, compliant operations
- 📈 Estate-wide operational visibility<br>
